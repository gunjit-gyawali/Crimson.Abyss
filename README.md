**Crimson.Abyss**

This is my first game made in Godot!
In Crimson.Abyss, you play as a vampire on a thrilling mission to collect all the orbs from the abyss.

How to Play
Move: Arrow keys
Use Cool Animation/Action: E key
Collect all the orbs to complete your quest!

Features
Play as a vampire
Explore the mysterious abyss
Fun animations and simple controls

Notes
This is a small, first project made in Godot, perfect for trying out your vampire powers and exploring game development!
